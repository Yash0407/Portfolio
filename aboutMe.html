About me!

Hello! My name is Yashvir Singh, and I am a student enrolled in the Computer Science program at York University. I completed my high school education in the Non-medical Science stream. One of my unique strengths is my ability to take the initiative to learn new skills and tackle any challenge that comes my way. I set high standards for myself and enjoy challenging myself with ambitious goal setting.

In my free time, I love to travel and explore new places, cultures, and cuisines. I am also a sports enthusiast and enjoy playing and watching games like cricket and wrestling. Besides, I am a music lover and often attend concerts to listen to my favorite artists. One of my favorite hobbies is equestrianism, which involves horse riding and caring for horses. It allows me to connect with nature and experience the beauty of horses. Overall, my diverse interests and hobbies, combined with my strengths and talents, make me a unique and well-rounded individual.

I grew up in Delhi, India, in a joint family, which taught me the value of community and cooperation. I learned how to live and work with others and how to be considerate of their needs and opinions. Additionally, experiencing the varied seasons taught me to be adaptable and resilient in the face of change. Overall, my upbringing in Delhi played a significant role in shaping my values and outlook on life.

My Vision

My vision as a software engineer is to develop innovative and sustainable technological solutions that tackle real-world problems and improve people's lives. I firmly believe that technology has the potential to make a significant positive impact on our society, and software engineering plays a critical role in shaping our future.

My ambition is to harness machine learning, AI, and data analytics to address pressing global issues, fostering collaboration among industries to serve the common good. By utilizing my skills in machine learning and data science, I aim to enhance lives and promote equitable access to resources. Sharing my passion with others, I hope to engage in conversations about our aspirations, contributing to a brighter future for all.

My favorite all-time inspiring teacher is my father, who taught me the importance of perseverance and never giving up on my dreams. His willingness to try new things and take on new opportunities has taught me the importance of embracing change and being open to new possibilities. His attributes of resilience and being comfortable out of his comfort zone have been a source of inspiration for me, and I continue to learn from him and strive to emulate his strength and determination in my own life.
